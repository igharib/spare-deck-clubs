Payments & Financials

No cash.
No chaos.
Full visibility.

Collect school fees, handle cafeteria payments, and track every transaction from a single dashboard. Parents pay via app; students tap or scan.

  • Fee Payments: Collect school fees through app or on campus with full balance visibility
  • Cashless Payments: Card, QR code, NFC wristband, or student ID, no cash required
  • Centralized Dashboard: All collections, balances, and reports in one place
  • 20+ Payment Gateways: Local and global, adaptable to any region
Cafeteria Experience

Fast, cash-free dining with pre-ordering and reduced queues.

Students pay with wristbands, QR codes, NFC cards, or student IDs. Parents pre-order meals. No cash, no queues, no hassle.

  • Wristband and QR payments: Tap-to-pay at any cafeteria station
  • Online pre-ordering: Parents select meals in advance from the app
  • Meal plans: Subscribe students to recurring meal plans
  • Handheld and Windows POS: Flexible hardware for any setup
Management & Operations

Real-time control over daily school life.

Track who's in class, who's been picked up, and what's happening on every bus route, with full parental monitoring built in.

  • Attendance Tracking: Real-time with SIS integration and instant parent alerts
  • Pickup Requests: Safe, controlled student pickup via parent app
  • Bus Services: Efficient transport management and scheduling
  • Parental Controls: Spending limits, restrictions, and real-time monitoring
Commerce & Marketplace

Convenience for parents.
New revenue for schools.

Give parents one app to order uniforms, books, trips, and meals. Schools get a centralized commerce channel that opens new revenue streams.

  • School Marketplace: Uniforms, books, supplies, events in one in-app store
  • 3rd Party Vendors: Trusted partners with on-campus or home delivery
  • 15M+ products sold: Proven at scale across schools in 8+ countries

Cafeteria Point of Sale

Fast checkout.
Zero queues.
Every station.

Spare's POS system turns any cafeteria into a frictionless, cashless experience. From handheld terminals to fixed kiosks, checkout is instant.

  • Handheld and Fixed POS: Windows-based terminals and mobile handhelds for any setup
  • Wristband, QR, and NFC: Students tap, scan, or wave to pay in seconds
  • Pre-order Integration: Orders placed ahead of time flow straight to the POS queue
  • Real-time Reporting: Live sales, item counts, and cashless reconciliation
