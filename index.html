The Problem

Multiple vendors. One club. Zero visibility.

Multiple outlets, no shared system. Spare brings everything under one roof.

  • Fragmented Payments: Each outlet runs its own register; members carry cash per vendor
  • Manual Fee Chasing: Renewals collected via cash, calls and WhatsApp with no automation
  • Lost Member Cards: Physical cards lost easily, unlinked from payments and spending data
  • Blind Attendance: Session tracking on paper, no real-time visibility for management
  • Booking Chaos: Phone/WhatsApp bookings lead to double-bookings and disputes
  • Scattered Comms: Updates split across WhatsApp, email, and notice boards
  • Data Loss at Turnover: Member history and financials lost when admins leave
Memberships & Fee Collection

Fee collection that doesn't need chasing.

Set up tiered membership plans (individual, family, junior, corporate), send renewal reminders automatically, and let members pay in one tap with a saved method. No spreadsheet. No manual follow-up.

  • Renewal Reminders: Push notification to member's app ahead of every renewal date
  • One-Tap Renewal: Members renew with a saved payment method in a single tap
  • Membership Plans: Individual, family, junior, social, and corporate tiers, all customizable
  • Self-Service Renewals: Members renew and upgrade directly from the app, no front-desk visit
  • Outstanding Balance Tracking: See who has paid, who hasn't, and send reminders in one click
  • Pay-at-Signup: Require payment at the moment of registration, eliminates the chasing cycle entirely
  • Centralized Membership Dashboard: Every member, every plan, every balance, visible in one screen
