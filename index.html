Payments & Financials

No cash.
No chaos.
Full visibility.

Collect school fees, handle cafeteria payments, and track every transaction from a single dashboard. Parents pay via app; students tap or scan.

  • Cashless Payments: Card, QR code, NFC wristband, or student ID, no cash required
  • Easy Recharge: Parents top up their child's balance through the dedicated app or at an on-campus terminal
  • Centralized Dashboard: All collections, balances, and reports in one place
  • Easy Bank Reconciliation: Clean, structured reports make matching transactions to bank statements fast and straightforward
  • 20+ Payment Gateways: Local and global, adaptable to any region
  • Service & Convenience Fees: Caterers can configure a per-transaction fee, fully controlled from the dashboard
  • Flexible Discounts: Apply discounts based on student type, membership, order value, payment method, or time of day
Cafeteria Experience

Fast, cash-free dining with pre-ordering and reduced queues.

Students pay with wristbands, QR codes, NFC cards, or student IDs. Parents pre-order meals. No cash, no queues, no hassle.

  • Wristband and QR payments: Tap-to-pay at any cafeteria station
  • Online pre-ordering: Parents select meals in advance from the app
  • Meal plans: Parents subscribe to weekly, monthly, or yearly plans from the app with full visibility on their child's meals
  • Handheld and Windows POS: Flexible hardware for any setup
  • Flexible student flows: Customizable checkout per campus, staffed counter, self-service kiosk, or a mix of both
  • Subsidized meals: Schools, governments, or third parties can top up student balances or fund free meal entitlements directly
